Promo Terms and Conditions

General Mechanics

  1. The promo is open to select UnionBank Credit Card principal cardholders who received the SMS and eDM notification.
  2. Promo period is from October 1, 2017 to January 31, 2018.
  3. Redemption period is from October 1, 2017 to March 31, 2018.
  4. Cardholder should make a minimum single-receipt transaction* of Php1,500 at any merchant to qualify for the promo.
    * A single-receipt transaction is a transaction covered by a single official receipt/sales invoice and a single credit card charge slip. Straight and installment retail transactions made in the Philippines and transacted in Philippine Pesos are included in this Promo. Non-retail transactions such as, but not limited to, Utility payments (cable, water, telephone, electricity), fees and charges, etc. as well as charge slips reflecting foreign currency transactions (including Duty Free Philippines transactions in US dollars), even if billed in Philippine Pesos, are excluded from the Promo. Transactions made at any gambling establishment such as casinos or those with no Point-of-Sale (POS) machine credit card charge slips such as, but not limited to, Auto charge, Balance Transfer, Retail Conversion, Retail Reimbursement, Cash Advance, Mail Order/Telephone Order transactions, etc. are likewise excluded from this Promo.
  5. A single purchase transaction requires only one approval. Splitting into smaller transaction sizes, and consequently, requiring multiple transaction approvals will not be allowed.
  6. Cardholder is entitled to receive one (1) Reference Code via SMS for every qualified and posted transactions.

  7. Cardholder is allowed to receive a maximum of five (5) Reference Codes within the promo period. Cardholder who reached the maximum limit will be notified via SMS and or email.

Redemption Mechanics

  1. To redeem, cardholder should present the SMS Voucher Code together with 1 Valid ID and UBP Credit Card to the participating merchant from October 1, 2017 to March 31, 2018.
  2. Participating establishment validates SMS and asks the cardholder to sign the monitoring sheet as proof of redemption.
  3. Upon receiving of the free gift the cardholder must allow the participating establishment to delete the SMS from his or her mobile phone.
  4. UnionBank, thru the frontliners of the participating establishments, reserves the right to refuse redemption of the free gift if the SMS is not from UnionBank’s official number.
  5. Cardholders without the SMS Voucher Code will not be allowed to redeem. SMS Voucher Codes may only be used once. The retail price/cost of the Items redeemed through multiple redemptions using one voucher code shall be charged to the Cardholder’s Card.
  6. A cardholder may redeem a maximum of 3 Voucher Codes per participating merchant per day.
  7. The free gift can only be redeemed by the principal cardholder and non-transferable.
  8. The free gift cannot be exchanged for cash, other products or discounts.
  9. All unused and unredeemed Reference Codes and Voucher Codes after the Redemption Period will be forfeited.
  10. In case of dispute with respect of UnionBank only and the customers' availment of promo, the decision of UnionBank with the concurrence of DTI shall be considered final. However, if dispute involves participating Merchant and its performance and/or delivery of products to customers, subject to the General Provisions set out above, the decision of UnionBank and participating Merchant with the concurrence of DTI shall be considered final.
